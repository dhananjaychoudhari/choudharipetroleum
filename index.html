Our Story
Three Decades of Fueling Industries

Founded in 1995, Choudhari Petroleum began as a regional fuel distributor with a single tanker and a commitment to honest, reliable service. Over thirty years, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted petroleum supply companies — without losing the personal touch that started it all.

"Our mission is not just to supply fuel — it is to power the ambitions of every business we serve, safely, reliably, and with absolute integrity."
— Mr. Ramesh Choudhari, Founder & Managing Director
